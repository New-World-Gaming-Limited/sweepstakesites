The Short Answer: No

Stake.us is not available to players in California. The state appears on the platform's restricted list, and CA residents cannot create accounts or access games. This is a platform-level decision, not a specific California law banning sweepstakes casinos.

Why California Is Restricted

California has one of the most complex gaming regulatory environments in the country. The state's 76 tribal nations hold significant influence over gaming policy through existing compacts, and any expansion of online play faces strong opposition from tribal gaming interests.

The ongoing AB 2863 discussions around tribal compact modernization have created uncertainty for sweepstakes platforms operating in the state. While AB 2863 does not directly regulate sweepstakes casinos, it reflects California's broader movement toward tighter control of online gaming categories.

This hostile regulatory climate has affected other platforms too. Pulsz, a competing sweepstakes casino, exited California in 2025 following the passage of AB 831, which imposed new compliance requirements on sweepstakes-style platforms. Stake.us has taken a similarly cautious approach by restricting CA access entirely rather than risking enforcement action.
