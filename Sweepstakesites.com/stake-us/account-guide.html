Two Registration Paths: Email vs. Social Login

Stake.us offers two ways to create an account. Each reaches the same destination, but the process differs slightly.

Option 1: Email Registration

This is the standard method described in the six steps above. You manually enter your email, username, password, date of birth, and state. This option gives you the most control over your login credentials and is recommended if you prefer a dedicated password for Stake.us.

Option 2: Social Login (Google, Facebook, or Twitch)

Instead of filling out the full form, you can click one of the social login buttons on the registration page:

  • Google: Uses your Google account email and authenticates through Google's login flow.
  • Facebook: Links your Facebook profile to your Stake.us account.
  • Twitch: Connects your Twitch account, which is popular among users who follow Stake.us streamers.

With social login, Stake.us pulls your name and email from the connected service. You will still need to provide your date of birth, state of residence, and complete identity verification. The promo code field is also available during social signup, so you can still enter MAXBET.

Which should you choose?

Social login is faster if you already use one of these services and want to avoid creating another password. Email registration is better if you want a standalone login that does not depend on a third-party account. Both methods receive the same welcome bonus and have the same account features.

Identity Verification (KYC)

Stake.us requires all accounts to complete identity verification before Stake Cash can be redeemed for prizes. This is a legal requirement for sweepstakes casinos operating in the United States and protects both the platform and its users.

What Documents Do You Need?

The verification process requires two items:

  1. Government-issued photo ID: A valid driver's license, state ID card, or US passport. The document must be unexpired and clearly legible in the photo.
  2. Selfie: A photo of yourself holding the same ID next to your face. Stake.us uses this to confirm that the person in the ID matches the account holder.

How to Submit

After registration, Stake.us displays a verification prompt. You can also access it later from Settings > Verification. Upload your ID photo and selfie through the on-screen prompts. Use good lighting and make sure all text on the ID is readable. Avoid using screenshots, scans, or edited images.

How Long Does Verification Take?

Most accounts are verified instantly through automated document checks. If the system flags your submission for manual review (blurry images, mismatched name, or an uncommon document format), it can take 24 to 72 hours. You will receive an email notification once the review is complete.

What Can You Do Before Verification?

Unverified accounts can still play with Gold Coins immediately after registration. However, you cannot redeem Stake Cash or access certain account features until KYC is complete. We recommend completing verification right away to avoid delays when you are ready to redeem.

Account Security and Two-Factor Authentication

Protecting your Stake.us account is important, especially if you have accumulated Stake Cash or linked payment methods. Stake.us offers several security features.

Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of protection to your login. Here is how to set it up:

  1. Download Google Authenticator (or any compatible TOTP app like Authy) on your phone.
  2. Log in to Stake.us and go to Settings > Security.
  3. Click Enable 2FA.
  4. Scan the QR code displayed on screen with your authenticator app.
  5. Enter the six-digit code generated by the app to confirm.
  6. Save the backup codes provided. Store them somewhere safe, not on your phone. If you lose access to your authenticator app, these codes are the only way to recover your account.

Once enabled, you will need to enter a code from your authenticator app every time you log in. This prevents unauthorized access even if someone obtains your password.

Password Recovery

If you forget your password, follow these steps:

  1. Click Forgot Password on the Stake.us login page.
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
  3. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the password reset email.
  4. Click the reset link and create a new password.

The reset link expires after 24 hours. If you use social login (Google, Facebook, or Twitch), your password is managed by that service. Reset it through their platform instead.

Additional Security Tips

  • Use a unique password that you do not reuse on other sites.
  • Never share your login credentials, promo codes, or 2FA backup codes with anyone claiming to be Stake.us support. Official support will never ask for your password.
  • Log out of shared or public devices after each session.
  • Review your login history under Settings > Security periodically to check for unrecognized sessions.
