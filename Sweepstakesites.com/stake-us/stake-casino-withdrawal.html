Stake Cash redemption on Stake.us follows a specific process. Before you can redeem, you must meet the minimum SC threshold, complete any applicable playthrough, and verify your identity.

  1. Ensure your account is fully verified (KYC complete).
  2. Navigate to Wallet → Redeem in your account.

Common Redemption Issues

  • Redemption locked: Usually because the minimum balance, rollover, or account verification requirements are not yet met.
  • Payment route mismatch: If you purchased GC with crypto, redemptions must go through crypto until your SC balance returns to zero.
  • Pending verification: KYC documents can take 24–72 hours to process.
  • Missing SC after purchase: Bonus SC is often credited after the first wager on the purchased GC, not immediately at purchase.
