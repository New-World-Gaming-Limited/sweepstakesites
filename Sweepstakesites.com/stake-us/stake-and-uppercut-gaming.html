The Two Signature Series

1000 Series (Zeus 1000, Phoenix 1000, Gladiator 1000, Samurai 1000): The headline mechanic is wild multipliers that scale up to 1000x during free spins. Zeus 1000 is the clear standout — it’s the most-played Uppercut title by a factor of two — but Phoenix 1000’s sticky wilds and Bonus Booster (2x bet for 3x trigger chance) and Bonus Hyper (10x bet for 20x trigger chance) modes give it serious depth.

Doubles Series (Dungeon Doubles, Olympus Doubles): These use a persistent reel multiplier system. Wilds landing on a reel double that reel’s multiplier, and the multipliers carry across spins during the bonus round. The ceiling is 1024x. Dungeon Doubles on its 5x4 grid is the more popular of the two, but both use the same core mechanic.
