Overview

Dorados is one of the more ambitious sweepstakes casino launches of 2026. Operated by Rafflefy Limited, the platform builds on a Blazesoft-style multi-currency model and combines it with a deep games library and a rewards economy that goes beyond standard Gold Coin and Sweeps Coin balances. The brand uses a gold-and-blue visual identity and positions itself in the same competitive tier as Pulsz, High 5, and Sweep Las Vegas.

Players move between four virtual currencies. Gold Coins are for casual play with no prize value. Gems function as the platform's Sweeps Coins equivalent and can be redeemed for prizes once playthrough is met. Elixirs are spent at an in-platform rewards market for game-related boosts and merchandise. Claw Machine Credits unlock a separate prize-pull mini-game and are awarded as part of first purchase offers and ongoing promotions.

Games and Providers

The Dorados game library exceeds 3,000 titles, putting it among the largest catalogs in the sweepstakes category. Slots dominate the lineup, with classic three-reel formats, modern Megaways-style titles, and bonus-buy options all represented. The table game section includes blackjack, baccarat, roulette, and poker variants. Live casino tables run through Evolution, giving Dorados an immersive option that few competing sweepstakes platforms can match.

Provider coverage is strong. Evolution powers the live casino, while Betsoft, NetEnt, Endorphina, Novomatic, Playson, and Hacksaw Gaming supply most of the slots catalog. Dorados also runs a small slate of exclusive slot games that are only available on the platform, giving regular players a reason to return.

Welcome Package

New players at Dorados receive 20,000 Gold Coins, 2 Gems, and 2 Elixirs at signup. No promo code is required and no purchase is needed to claim the bonus. After signup, the platform surfaces a 150 percent first purchase offer that bundles 250,000 Gold Coins, 25 Gems, 1 Elixir, and 1 Claw Machine Credit for $9.99. The first purchase offer is optional, but the headline value per dollar is among the strongest in the category.

The 1x playthrough on Gems is the same standard requirement applied across most sweepstakes casinos. Players need to play through the Gem balance one time before it becomes eligible for redemption, which keeps the path to a first cash prize relatively short.

Redemption Process

Once playthrough is met, Dorados players can redeem Gems for cash prizes. The minimum redemption is 50 Gems, which converts to a $50 cash prize. The platform supports three redemption methods: bank wire, Visa, and Mastercard. Bank wire payouts typically take 3 to 5 business days. Card-based redemptions are usually faster.

Players are required to complete identity verification before their first redemption, which is standard across the sweepstakes category. Verification is generally completed within 24 to 48 hours when documents are clear.

State Availability

Dorados is available in roughly 39 US states at launch. The platform is restricted in California, Connecticut, Idaho, Louisiana, Michigan, Montana, Nevada, New Jersey, New York, Tennessee, and Washington. Most of those states either have an existing sweepstakes ban, an active enforcement action, or a regulated iGaming market that limits sweepstakes operators. Players should confirm their state is eligible before creating an account.

Verdict

Dorados earns a 4.5 out of 5.0 score in our evaluation. The 3,000+ game library, tier-one provider lineup, 1x playthrough, and aggressive welcome offer combine to create one of the strongest sweepstakes casino launches we have reviewed in 2026. The multi-currency model adds some complexity, but the rewards economy gives long-term players genuine reasons to keep an account active beyond a single welcome bonus.

The platform is best suited for players who want a deep games catalog, modern provider coverage, and a competitive ongoing promotional schedule. Players in the 11 restricted states will need to look elsewhere.
