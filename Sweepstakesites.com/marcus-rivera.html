About Marcus

Marcus Rivera is a South Side Chicago guy who started his career in fintech compliance, spending three years as a compliance analyst at an early-stage payments startup where he learned every regulation from KYC/AML to state money transmitter licensing. He then joined Gambling.com, one of the largest iGaming affiliate networks in the world, where he spent two years as a regulatory content specialist covering state-by-state gambling legislation, operator licensing requirements, and consumer protection frameworks across US markets. That combination of hands-on compliance work and editorial experience is exactly what the sweepstakes space needs.
