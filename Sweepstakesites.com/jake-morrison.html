About Jake

Jake Morrison grew up in Birmingham, Alabama, where Friday night meant high school football and Saturday meant his granddad's poker game on the back porch. After earning his journalism degree from UNLV, he moved to Vegas and never looked back. Jake started his career at BallIsLife.com, covering sports betting culture and the intersection of sports and online gaming. He then moved to Covers.com, one of the most established sports betting media properties in North America, where he spent three years as a senior writer covering regulated gambling markets, sportsbook reviews, and promotional analysis. After eight-plus years in the industry, Jake founded SweepstakeSites to bring the same editorial rigor to the sweepstakes casino space. He personally signs up for every platform before the team writes a word about it. If the bonus doesn't work the way they say it does, y'all are gonna hear about it. Jake runs the editorial calendar, manages the verification schedule, and still tests every promo code himself because he doesn't trust anybody else to do it right.
