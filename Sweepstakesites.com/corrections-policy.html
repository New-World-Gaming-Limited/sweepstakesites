How SweepstakeSites handles errors, corrections, and updates to published content.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

We take factual accuracy seriously. When we get something wrong, we fix it quickly and transparently.

Types of Corrections

  • Minor corrections: Typos, formatting issues, or outdated dates. These are fixed immediately without a formal correction notice.
  • Material corrections: Incorrect bonus amounts, wrong promo codes, inaccurate state availability, or misleading claims. These are corrected promptly with an update note on the affected page.
  • Retractions: In the rare case that an entire article contains fundamentally flawed information, we will retract and republish with a clear notice.

How to Report an Error

Contact us through our contact page with:

  • The URL of the page containing the error
  • A description of what appears incorrect
  • If possible, a link to the correct source

Response Timeline

We acknowledge error reports within 24 hours and aim to publish corrections within 48 hours of confirmation. Critical errors affecting user financial decisions (wrong bonus amounts, incorrect promo codes) are prioritized and corrected same-day.
