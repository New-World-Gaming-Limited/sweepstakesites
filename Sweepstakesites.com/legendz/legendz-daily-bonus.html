Legendz Daily Login Rewards

Legendz rewards players who log in each day with free Gold Coins and, occasionally, Sweeps Coins. The daily bonus is a simple way to build your coin balance without making any purchases. Here is how it works as of March 2026.

How Daily Bonuses Work

Every time you log into Legendz, you can claim a daily bonus. The reward is available once per day and resets at midnight. Simply log in, look for the daily bonus prompt, and click to claim your free coins.

Daily Bonus Structure

The daily bonus follows a tiered structure that rewards consecutive logins. The longer your login streak, the larger the daily reward becomes.

Login DayTypical Reward
Day 1Gold Coins bonus
Day 2Slightly larger GC bonus
Day 3Increased GC bonus
Day 4Larger GC bonus
Day 5GC bonus + small SC bonus

Day 7Largest weekly reward (GC + SC)

Exact amounts vary and are subject to change. The key point is that later days in the streak provide better rewards, and SC bonuses typically appear on milestone days.

Streak Mechanics

  • Consecutive days matter. Missing a day resets your streak back to Day 1. Consistency is what drives the bigger rewards.
  • One claim per day. You cannot claim the bonus multiple times in the same day. Log in, claim, play, and come back tomorrow.
  • Timezone based. The daily reset occurs at midnight based on the Legendz server time. Plan your login accordingly.

Maximizing Your Daily Rewards

  • Set a reminder. A daily phone alarm or calendar notification ensures you do not miss a day and break your streak.
  • Add Legendz to your home screen. Quick access via your phone's home screen makes the daily check-in effortless. See our mobile guide for setup instructions.
  • Combine with other bonuses. Daily bonuses stack with the welcome bonus and first purchase match. Every free coin adds up.

How Daily Bonuses Compare

What sets Legendz apart is that any SC received through daily bonuses carries the same 1x playthrough requirement, meaning your free SC is easier to convert into redeemable value here than on platforms with higher playthrough.
