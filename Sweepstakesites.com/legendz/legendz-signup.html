How to Sign Up for Legendz

Creating a Legendz account takes about two minutes. This guide walks you through each step of the registration process as of March 2026.

Step-by-Step Registration

1

Visit Legendz

Go to Legendz.com using our link. Click the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the homepage.

2

Enter Your Details

Fill in your name, email address, date of birth, and create a password. You must be 21 or older to register.

3

Enter Promo Code

In the promo code field, type MAXBET. This activates the welcome bonus of 500 GC + 3 SC. If the field is pre-filled from our link, you are all set.

4

Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from Legendz. Click the link to confirm your account. Your 500 GC + 3 SC will be credited once verification is complete.

5

Start Playing

Log in and explore the game library, social sportsbook, and live casino. Your welcome bonus is ready to use immediately.

Requirements to Register

  • Age: You must be 21 years or older.
  • Location: You must be in an eligible U.S. state. Legendz operates in 37 states. See our legal states guide for the full list.
  • One account per person. Legendz does not allow duplicate accounts. Attempting to create multiple accounts can result in all accounts being closed.
  • Valid email address. You need a working email for verification and account recovery.

What Happens After Sign-Up

Once your account is active, you have immediate access to:

  • The full game library of 400+ slots, table games, and live casino
  • The social sportsbook
  • Daily login rewards
  • The first purchase match (100% up to $100) when you decide to buy coins

Your 500 GC lets you play any game in fun mode. The 3 SC can be used on any game and is eligible for redemption after 1x playthrough.

Troubleshooting Registration

  • Not receiving the verification email? Check your spam or junk folder. If it is not there, use the "Resend" option on the login page.
