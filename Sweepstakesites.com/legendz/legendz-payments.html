Identity Verification (KYC)

Legendz follows standard KYC procedures required by sweepstakes regulations. Before your first redemption, you will need to provide:

  • A photo of a government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement dated within 90 days)
  • The name on your account must match your ID

Verification typically completes within 24 to 48 hours. Once verified, subsequent redemptions process without additional document requests.

Common Issues

  • Card declined: Try online banking or Skrill. Some banks restrict sweepstakes transactions.
  • Redemption pending: First-time redemptions may take longer due to KYC review. Allow up to 5 business days.
  • SC not redeemable: Ensure you have met the 1x playthrough requirement. Check your account dashboard for progress.
