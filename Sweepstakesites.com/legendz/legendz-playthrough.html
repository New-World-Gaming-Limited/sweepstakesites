Legendz 1x Playthrough Explained

Legendz 1x Playthrough: What It Means

Legendz applies a 1x playthrough requirement to all Sweeps Coin bonuses. This is the lowest playthrough in the sweepstakes casino industry as of March 2026. This guide explains why it matters and how it compares to other platforms.

How Playthrough Works

A playthrough requirement (also called a playing requirement) is the number of times you must play your bonus Sweeps Coins before you can redeem any winnings. With 1x playthrough, you play each SC just once, then you are free to redeem.

Here is an example. You receive 10 SC as a bonus. With 1x playthrough, you need to place $10 in total plays. After that, any SC remaining in your balance (whether you won or lost) becomes eligible for redemption.

Why 1x Playthrough Is a Big Deal

The math is straightforward. Every time you play, the house has an edge. A slot with 96% RTP returns $0.96 for every $1 played, on average. The more times you cycle your balance through games, the more you lose to the house edge.

  • 1x playthrough (Legendz): You lose approximately 4% of your bonus to house edge before redeeming.
  • 3x playthrough (Stake.us): You lose approximately 12% of your bonus to house edge before redeeming.
  • 5x playthrough (some platforms): You lose approximately 20% of your bonus to house edge before redeeming.

At 1x playthrough, your bonus retains most of its value. At 3x or 5x, a significant chunk is eaten by the house edge before you can ever redeem.

Which Games Count Toward Playthrough?

On Legendz, all games contribute 100% toward the playthrough requirement. This includes slots, table games, live casino, and the social sportsbook. Some platforms exclude certain games or count them at reduced rates (e.g., table games counting 10%), but Legendz keeps it simple: play anything, and it counts fully.

Tracking Your Playthrough

Your account dashboard shows your current playthrough progress. Once the requirement is met, your SC balance becomes fully redeemable. There is no manual action needed: the system automatically unlocks redemption eligibility once you hit the threshold.

Bottom line: The 1x playthrough at Legendz means your bonus SC is worth close to face value. On a 3x platform, a 10 SC bonus is really worth about 8.8 SC after accounting for the house edge over three rounds of playing.
