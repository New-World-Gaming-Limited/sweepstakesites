Sweeps Coins and Gold Coins Explained

Legendz operates on a dual-currency system common across sweepstakes platforms.

  • Gold Coins (GC) are the free-play currency. They have no cash value but let you play every game on the platform. You receive them through purchases, daily logins, and promotions.
  • Sweeps Coins (SC) are the prize-eligible currency. After meeting the 1x playthrough requirement, SC can be redeemed for prizes at a rate of 1 SC = $1 USD. You cannot purchase SC directly. They are included as a bonus with GC packages and earned through free promotions.

This dual-currency model is what allows Legendz to operate as a sweepstakes platform rather than requiring a traditional gaming license. It also means you always have a path to free play through daily login bonuses, mail-in requests, and promotional offers.
