Add to Home Screen (PWA)

For the best mobile experience, add Legendz to your home screen. This creates an app-like shortcut that launches the site without the browser address bar, giving you a cleaner, full-screen experience.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Legendz.com in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."
  4. Name the shortcut and tap "Add."

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Legendz.com in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right.
  3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app."
  4. Confirm the name and tap "Add."

Once added, tapping the Legendz icon on your home screen launches the site in a standalone window. It looks and feels like a native app.
