Understanding RTP on Legendz

RTP (Return to Player) represents the theoretical percentage a slot pays back over millions of spins. Most slots on Legendz sit between 95% and 97%. Higher RTP does not guarantee short-term results, but it does mean lower house edge over extended play. Check the game info screen within each slot to see its specific RTP.
