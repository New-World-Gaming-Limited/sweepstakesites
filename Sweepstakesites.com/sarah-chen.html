About Sarah

Sarah Chen is based in Seattle, where she does most of her game analysis fueled by an alarming amount of cold brew from the shop down on Pike Street. She studied Mathematics at UC Berkeley - the kind of person who reads RTP documentation for fun. Before joining SweepstakeSites, Sarah spent two years at FreeTips.com writing game strategy guides and probability breakdowns for casino players across the UK and US markets. She then moved to Covers.com, where she spent two years as a data analyst crunching return-to-player metrics and house edge calculations across hundreds of online casino titles. That four-year track record of hands-on RTP analysis is what makes her guides different. Sarah writes all the game guides and strategy content for SweepstakeSites. Her rule: if someone on YouTube tells you they found a "guaranteed strategy," they're lying. She'll show you what the numbers actually say instead. Her desk has three monitors and a whiteboard covered in probability trees. The rain outside doesn't bother her - she barely notices it.
