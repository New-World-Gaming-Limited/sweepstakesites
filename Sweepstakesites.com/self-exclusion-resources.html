Self-exclusion tools and resources for sweepstakes casino players who need to take a break.

What Is Self-Exclusion?

Self-exclusion is a voluntary program that allows you to ban yourself from gambling platforms for a set period. Once enrolled, you cannot access your account or create new ones during the exclusion period.

How to Self-Exclude on Stake.us

Stake.us offers built-in responsible gaming tools in your account settings:

  • Deposit limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly purchase limits.
  • Loss limits: Cap your losses over a set period.
  • Session time limits: Get reminded or logged out after a set duration.
  • Cool-off period: Temporarily restrict your account for 24 hours to 6 months.
  • Permanent self-exclusion: Permanently close your account.

National Resources

  • National Council on Problem Gambling (NCPG): Call 1-800-522-4700 or text 1-800-522-4700. Available 24/7.
  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: ncpgambling.org
  • Gamblers Anonymous: gamblersanonymous.org
  • SAMHSA Helpline: 1-800-662-4357 (free, confidential, 24/7)

State-Level Resources

Many states operate their own self-exclusion registries. Contact your state gaming commission or visit the NCPG State Affiliates page to find local support.

Signs You May Need Help

  • Spending more time or money than you planned
  • Chasing losses with bigger bets
  • Borrowing money to play
  • Neglecting responsibilities because of gambling
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not playing

If any of these apply, please reach out. The helpline is free, confidential, and available around the clock: 1-800-522-4700.
