Executive Summary

WOW Vegas is one of the larger sweepstakes casinos in the US with over 2 million registered players. Operated by MW Services Limited (Gibraltar) and WOW Entertainment Limited, the same parent company behind Rolla, it offers 1,500+ games with a focus on slots, plus limited table games and some live dealer options. Available in approximately 35 US states, WOW Vegas uses WOW Coins (its play-for-fun currency) and Sweeps Coins with a 1x playthrough requirement. What We Like

  • Extensive daily promotions. Lunchtime Loot, Happy Hour (6-8 PM ET), Race Rewards, Paris' Lucky 6, and weekly missions give you multiple reasons to log in every day. Few competitors match this promotion frequency.
  • Large player base. With 2M+ registered players, WOW Vegas has proven market traction and a track record of reliable operation since 2021.
  • WOW Jackpots. Progressive jackpot games add excitement that many sweepstakes casinos lack. The jackpot pools grow across the player base.
  • 1x playthrough on Sweeps Coins. Play through your SC just once before redeeming. This is the best rate in the industry.
  • Lower gift card redemption minimum. Gift card redemptions start at just 25 SC (via PrizeOut equivalent), which is lower than many competitors.
  • MW Services pedigree. The same operator runs Rolla, giving WOW Vegas an established infrastructure in the sweepstakes space.

What Falls Short

  • Limited game diversity. While the 1,500+ game count is solid, the library is heavily weighted toward slots. Table game and live dealer options remain limited compared to platforms like Chumba or High 5.
  • No mobile app. Browser-based only. No dedicated iOS or Android app means no push notifications and potentially a less optimized mobile experience.
  • Restricted in 17 states. Available in roughly 35 states, which is fewer than some competitors. States like PA, NY, and MI are excluded.
  • Cash redemption minimum is 100 SC. If you want cash instead of gift cards, you need to accumulate 100 SC first. The gift card minimum of 25 SC is more accessible.
  • 3-5 business day redemption processing. Some competitors process redemptions faster. The wait can feel long compared to platforms with 1-2 day turnarounds.

Who It Is For

Players who enjoy slots and want a platform packed with daily promotions. If you like logging in to check what new offer is running, WOW Vegas delivers more ongoing promotions than most competitors. The WOW Jackpots add a progressive element that slots enthusiasts appreciate, and the 2M+ player base means the platform is well-established and reliable.

Who Should Look Elsewhere

Players who want a diverse table game library, a dedicated mobile app, or faster redemption times should explore other options. If you are in one of the 17 restricted states (including PA, NY, NJ, and MI), WOW Vegas is not available to you. Consider Stake.us for broader state availability or Legendz for social sportsbook features.
