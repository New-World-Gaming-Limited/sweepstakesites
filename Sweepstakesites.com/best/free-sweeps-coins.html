Frequently Asked Questions

Are free Sweeps Coins really free?

Yes. Under sweepstakes law, platforms must provide free methods of entry. Signup bonuses, daily logins, mail-in requests, and social media giveaways all provide SC at no cost. You never need to make a purchase to play.

Can I redeem prizes from free SC?

Absolutely. Free SC works exactly the same as purchased SC. Once you meet the playthrough requirement (1x at most platforms, 3x at Stake.us), you can request a redemption regardless of whether you purchased any coins.

How do mail-in entries work?

Write a handwritten request including your full name, account email, and the platform name, then mail it to the address listed in the platform's Terms of Service. Most platforms send your free SC within 7-10 business days of receiving the request.
