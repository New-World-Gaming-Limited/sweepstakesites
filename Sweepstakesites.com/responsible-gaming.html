Responsible Gaming

Gaming should be entertainment, not a source of stress. SweepstakeSites is committed to promoting responsible play.

Setting Limits

Before you play, decide how much time and money you are comfortable spending. Stick to those limits. Most sweepstakes platforms, including Stake.us, provide tools to set session time reminders and spending caps.

Warning Signs

  • Spending more than you can afford
  • Chasing losses
  • Gaming interfering with work, relationships, or daily life
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not playing
  • Borrowing money to play

Resources

  • National Council on Problem Gambling: 1-800-522-4700 or ncpgambling.org
  • Gamblers Anonymous: gamblersanonymous.org
  • SAMHSA Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
