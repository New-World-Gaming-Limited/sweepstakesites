Game Library

Game libraries are comparable in size. Shuffle.us offers 500+ titles and Stake.us has 550+. Both include slots, table games, live dealer, and proprietary Originals. Payments

Stake.us wins on flexibility. Stake.us accepts both cryptocurrency and fiat payments (credit cards, bank transfers), making it accessible to everyone. Shuffle.us is crypto-only, great if you're already in the crypto ecosystem, but a dealbreaker if you prefer traditional payment methods. Virtually a tie. Stake.us is available in 32+ states while Shuffle.us covers 31+ states. Shuffle restricts 19 states including FL, GA, NJ, NY, and PA. Stake.us restrictions include WA, NY, NV, ID, and KY among others. Check both sites' terms pages for the latest state lists.

What Could Be Better

Neither platform is perfect. Here's what each needs to improve:

The Verdict

Choose Stake.us if you want the bigger welcome bonus, prefer fiat payment options, or value a platform with a longer track record and mainstream brand recognition (UFC, Everton FC partnerships).

Choose Shuffle.us if you're a crypto-native user who wants the largest game library in the sweepstakes space, provably fair Shuffle Originals, and tournament-style competition.

For most players, Stake.us remains the safer, more accessible choice. Shuffle.us is the better pick for crypto enthusiasts who prioritize game variety over bonus size.
