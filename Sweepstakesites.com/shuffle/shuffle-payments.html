Crypto-Only Payments

Shuffle.us is a crypto-native sweepstakes casino. Unlike most competitors that accept credit cards, bank transfers, or e-wallets, Shuffle operates exclusively with cryptocurrency. The three supported currencies are Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Litecoin (LTC). This applies to both purchases and redemptions.

How Purchases Work

  1. Log in to your Shuffle.us account and navigate to the purchase section.
  2. Select your preferred cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, or LTC).
  3. Shuffle.us generates a unique wallet address for your purchase.
  4. Send crypto from your personal wallet or exchange to the provided address.
  5. Once the blockchain confirms the transaction, funds appear in your Shuffle.us balance.

Confirmation times depend on the cryptocurrency and network congestion. Bitcoin typically requires 1 to 3 confirmations, while Litecoin is generally faster.

How Redemptions Work

  1. Go to the redemption section in your account.
  2. Select the cryptocurrency you want to redeem.
  3. Enter your external wallet address and the amount.
  4. Confirm the redemption. Shuffle.us processes the request and sends the transaction to the blockchain.

Redemption processing is typically fast, with most requests handled within minutes to a few hours. There are no intermediate banking steps since everything stays on-chain.

Why Crypto-Only?

Operating exclusively in crypto offers several practical benefits:

  • Faster transactions. No waiting 3 to 5 business days for bank transfers. Most purchases and redemptions complete in minutes.
  • Lower fees. Crypto transactions generally carry lower processing fees than credit card or bank wire payments.
  • Privacy. Crypto transactions do not appear on bank statements or credit card bills.
  • Global access. No currency conversion issues for players in eligible states.

What If I Do Not Have Crypto?

If you are new to cryptocurrency, you will need to purchase some before purchasing on Shuffle.us. Popular exchanges like Coinbase, Kraken, and Cash App make it straightforward to buy BTC, ETH, or LTC with a debit card or bank transfer. See our Bitcoin purchase guide for a step-by-step walkthrough.
