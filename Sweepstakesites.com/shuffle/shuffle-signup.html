How to Sign Up for Shuffle.us

Creating Your Shuffle.us Account

Signing up for Shuffle.us takes a few minutes. The process is straightforward, but since this is a crypto-native platform, there are a couple of differences compared to traditional sweepstakes casinos. Here is everything you need to know.

What You Need

  • Email address: A valid email for account verification.
  • Age verification: You must be 21 or older.
  • Eligible location: Shuffle.us is available in 31 US states. Check the legal states page to confirm your state is eligible.
  • Crypto wallet (optional for signup): You do not need a crypto wallet to create your account or claim the no-purchase bonus. However, you will need one (BTC, ETH, or LTC) if you plan to make purchases later.

Step-by-Step Registration

1

Visit Shuffle.us

Go to Shuffle.us using our link to ensure the MAXBET code is applied.

2

Click Sign Up

Click the registration button on the homepage. You will see a form asking for basic information.

3

Fill in Your Details

Enter your email, choose a username, and create a strong password. If there is a promo code field, enter MAXBET.

4

Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from Shuffle.us. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.

5

Complete Verification

Depending on your state, you may need to verify your identity with a government-issued ID. This is standard for sweepstakes platforms.

After Registration

Once your account is active, you will receive 25,000 GC + 1 SC as your no-purchase welcome bonus. These are credited automatically and let you start playing immediately. Gold Coins are for free play, while Sweeps Coins are the prize-eligible currency.

Setting Up a Crypto Wallet

If you plan to purchase funds beyond the welcome bonus, you will need a cryptocurrency wallet. Here are the easiest options:

  • Coinbase: The most popular option. Buy and send BTC, ETH, or LTC directly from the app.
  • Cash App: Supports Bitcoin purchases and transfers. Great if you already use it.
  • MetaMask: A browser-based wallet for Ethereum. Good for players who prefer ETH.

See our Bitcoin purchase guide for a detailed walkthrough of purchasing and transferring crypto.

Common Signup Issues

  • Verification email not arriving? Check your spam folder. If it still does not appear, request a new verification email from the login page.
  • Restricted state? If you are in one of the 19 restricted states, you will not be able to complete registration. See the full list of restricted states.
  • Already have an account? Shuffle.us allows one account per person. Creating duplicate accounts may result in suspension.
