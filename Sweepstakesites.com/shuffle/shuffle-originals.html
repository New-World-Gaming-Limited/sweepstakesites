Shuffle Originals: Provably Fair Games

What Are Shuffle Originals?

Shuffle Originals are in-house games built and operated directly by the Shuffle.us platform. Unlike third-party slots or table games from external providers, these titles are designed from the ground up with provably fair technology. every play you place on an Original can be independently verified, giving you mathematical proof that the outcome was not manipulated.

Available Original Games

Dice

Set a target number between 1 and 100, then roll. If the result lands above or below your target (depending on your selection), you win. The payout adjusts dynamically based on the probability you choose. Higher risk means higher reward. Dice is the simplest Original and a good starting point for understanding provably fair mechanics.

Mines

A grid-based game where you uncover tiles while avoiding hidden mines. Each safe tile you reveal increases your multiplier. You can redeem at any point or keep going for a bigger payout. The number of mines is configurable, letting you control the risk level. With fewer mines, payouts are smaller but safer. With more mines, the multiplier climbs quickly.

Plinko

Drop a ball from the top of a triangular peg board and watch it bounce down to a payout slot at the bottom. You can choose the number of rows (affecting volatility) and the risk level. Plinko is entirely luck-based but visually engaging, and the provably fair system ensures each drop is genuinely random.

Limbo

Set a target multiplier and place your play. The game generates a random multiplier. If the generated number meets or exceeds your target, you win. Limbo is fast-paced, often completing rounds in under a second. High target multipliers offer massive potential payouts but hit less frequently.

Crash

A multiplier starts at 1x and climbs continuously. Your job is to redeem before the multiplier "crashes" back to zero. The longer you wait, the higher the potential payout, but if the crash happens before you redeem, you lose your play. Crash combines timing with risk tolerance and is one of the most popular Originals.

How Provably Fair Works

Before each round, the server generates a hashed seed. After the round completes, both the server seed and client seed are revealed. You can then run a verification algorithm to confirm the outcome was determined before you placed your play. This eliminates the possibility of the house altering results after the fact. Shuffle.us provides a built-in verification tool so you can check any play directly from your game history.

Why Originals Matter

Provably fair games solve a fundamental trust problem. Traditional online casino games rely on third-party audits and certifications. Originals let you verify outcomes yourself, every single time. The house edge on Shuffle Originals is typically lower than on standard slots, ranging from 1% to 4% depending on the game and settings. For players who value transparency and favorable odds, Originals are the strongest offering on the platform.
