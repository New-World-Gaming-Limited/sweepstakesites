About SweepstakeSites

We test sweepstakes casinos, verify promo codes, and tell you what's actually worth your time.

SweepstakeSites (sweepstakesites.com) is an independent resource for sweepstakes casino players in the United States. We publish promo code guides, platform reviews, bonus comparisons, and educational content about how sweepstakes casinos work under US law.

What We Do

We test sweepstakes casinos so you know what you're getting into before you sign up. Every promo code on this site has been personally entered by one of our editors. Every bonus amount has been verified against the live platform. If something changes, we update it - usually within 48 hours.

How We Work

Every article on SweepstakeSites goes through the same process:

  1. Hands-on testing: The assigned author creates an account, enters the promo code, plays the games, and documents what actually happens - not what the marketing page says happens.
  2. Fact-check & edit: A second team member reviews the piece for accuracy. If the numbers don't match the platform, it goes back for correction.
  3. Ongoing monitoring: Published articles go into our weekly audit rotation. Bonus amounts change constantly in this industry, and stale info helps nobody.

Affiliate Disclosure

We make money through affiliate partnerships - that's how we keep the lights on and the site free. But here's the thing: our editorial team writes independently. Reviews, rankings, and recommendations are based on our testing, not on who pays us the most. We only recommend platforms we've personally used and would use ourselves.
